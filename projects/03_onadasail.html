Onadasail

Onadasail was a comprehensive project where I was entrusted with the end-to-end responsibility of creating a cohesive brand identity, designing the UI/UX, developing the WordPress website, and implementing SEO/SEM strategies.

The primary objective was to establish an effective online presence to start selling sailing experiences. Despite the multifaceted challenges, the focus remained on delivering a seamless online experience that generates reassurance for visitors, supported by responsive design to cater to a global audience.

See how it works

UI/UX Design & Development, Branding.

Featuring a spacious and responsive design, the onadasail.com homepage is crafted to showcase stunning images and videos of the products. The layout is designed to capture attention while maintaining user engagement, highlighting the brand's commitment to visual excellence.

The WordPress platform was chosen for its flexibility and scalability, allowing for easy content management and future expansion of the online store.⁤

From color schemes to typography, every detail was tailored to reflect the brand's identity and enhance user experience across different devices.⁤
