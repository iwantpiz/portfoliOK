Onadasail

Establishing a professional online image that inspires user trust.

I led the Onadasail project, handling brand identity, UI/UX design, WordPress development, and SEO/SEM.

The goal was to create an effective online presence for selling sailing experiences, focusing on a seamless, responsive design that reassures visitors and appeals to a global audience.

Key Objectives:

  • Custom design from color schemes to typography to reflect the brand's identity across all devices.
  • Building Trust to establish a professional online presence that fosters user trust.
  • Boosting Sales on increasing direct sales by minimizing reliance on third-party platforms.

See how it works

UI/UX Design & Development, Branding.

Featuring a spacious and responsive design, the homepage is crafted to showcase stunning images and videos of the products. The layout is designed to capture attention while maintaining user engagement, highlighting the brand's commitment to visual excellence.

The WordPress platform was chosen for its flexibility and scalability, allowing for easy content management and future expansion of the online store.⁤

From color schemes to typography, every detail was tailored to reflect the brand's identity and enhance user experience across different devices.⁤
