Onadasail

Establishing a professional online image that inspires user trust.

Overview

Onadasail appears to be a sailing association based in the Balearic Islands, also known as Onada Vela. They are involved in organizing sailing events, training sessions, and promoting the sport of sailing in the Mediterranean.

Problem

The goal was to create an effective online presence for selling sailing experiences, with a responsive design that reassures visitors and appeals to a global audience.

Solution

  • Custom design from color schemes to typography to reflect the brand's identity across all devices.
  • Enhance user engagement to establish a professional online presence that fosters user trust.
  • Boosting sales on increasing direct sales by minimizing reliance on third-party platforms.

The aim was to establish a professional online presence that fosters trust by prioritizing a clean, user-friendly interface, clear messaging, and reliable functionality. This was achieved through transparent communication, easy-to-navigate designs, and trust signals such as customer testimonials.

The WordPress platform was chosen for its flexibility, scalability and ease of use, allowing for easy content management and future expansion of the online store.⁤

From color schemes to typography, every detail was tailored to reflect the brand's identity and enhance user experience across different devices. The redesign led to a 20% increase in online bookings and garnered positive user feedback for its intuitive navigation and appealing design.⁤
