Nok Nok

Rebranded to boost conversions.

The project focused on re-designing the Nok Nok London website to increase sales conversions. Despite high traffic from social media, the existing site was not effectively converting visitors into customers.

The goal was to create a more engaging, user-friendly platform that would enhance the shopping experience and boost sales.

Key Objectives:

  • Refreshing the brand’s visual to better resonate with the target audience.
  • Simplifying navigation, improving site speed, and making the purchasing process more intuitive.
  • Modernizing the design to be more visually appealing and functional, and to better adapt to the brand's needs.
  • Implementing effective CTAs, streamlining the checkout process, and utilizing customer engagement strategies to drive sales.

See how it works

UI/UX Design & Development, Branding.

The outdated and cluttered design coupled with inconsistent branding elements, results in complicated and non-intuitive navigation, making it difficult for users to find products. This inconsistent and error-ridden design further increases user frustration, making it harder for them to complete purchases smoothly.

Additionally, the lack of user-friendly features such as quick view, along with a lengthy and complicated checkout process, leads to a poor shopping experience in both Desktop & Mobile.⁤

The new design features a sophisticated, modern look with consistent branding elements that create a cohesive and professional appearance. Simplified menu structure with clear categories and subcategories, making it easier for users to find products.

Intuitive layout with easy access to important features. Fully responsive design that ensures a consistent shopping experience across all devices.⁤ Clear and strategically placed call-to-actions that guide users towards making a purchase. Features such as email sign-ups, pop-up offers, and abandoned cart reminders to encourage conversions.

After creating the low-fidelity wireframes, a survey was conducted to gather feedback and insights from potential users regarding the app's layout, design, and functionality. By analyzing the Wireframe Survey Results, valuable insights were gained to refine and improve the app's design, ensuring it meets the needs and preferences of the target audience.
