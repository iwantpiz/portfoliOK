Psicoline

Delivering a user-friendly, private platform for accessible mental health support.

Psicoline is a progressive web app for women in Spain facing barriers to face-to-face psychological consultations.

The goal was to create an accessible, user-friendly platform that offers a more private, comfortable alternative to traditional online services, simplifying the process of seeking help.

Key Objectives:

  • Provide a convenient alternative to traditional face-to-face psychological consultations.
  • Offer a private and comfortable environment for women to discuss their problems.
  • Simplify and streamline the process of seeking psychological help online.

The project has not been launched online.

UX Research, UI/UX Design.

After conducting a comprehensive analysis of existing online psychological services and apps available in Spanish to identify strengths/weaknesses and gaps in the market, a detailed user persona was created to represent the target audience⁤. Challenges: lack of time for face-to-face consultations, feelings of shame and discomfort discussing personal issues with strangers.

A user journey map was developed to visualize the steps the User Personas take from discovering the app to completing a session with a psychologist. ⁤

6

Based on the user journey and card sorting results, a user flow was created to outline the steps Marta would take while using the app. Low-fidelity wireframes were developed to visualize the app's layout and structure, focusing on functionality and user flow.⁤

Market requirements: privacy & security, user-friendliness (clean, intuitive interface), accessibility (for disabilities or older women), tailored support (customized experience based on specific mental health needs), ease of communication (different ways to connect to cater to different comfort levels), retention (non-intrusive notifications, and progress tracking to give a sense of control and accomplishment), resources and self-care tools.

After creating the low-fidelity wireframes, a survey was conducted to gather feedback and insights from potential users regarding the app's layout, design, and functionality. By analyzing the Wireframe Survey Results, valuable insights were gained to refine and improve the app's design, ensuring it meets the needs and preferences of the target audience: ⁤ inclusivity (design should consider different backgrounds), emotional design (use of calm, soft color palettes and friendly, approachable visual elements to reduce anxiety and make users feel comfortable).
